How to Reach Us

Traveling to UBNHS has never been easier. Here's how:

By Jeepney

Take any jeepney heading to Central Bicutan, and ask the driver to drop you off near the UBNHS campus.

By Bus

Board a bus that passes through Taguig City and alight near Central Bicutan landmarks.

By Grab or Taxi

Simply pin "Central Bicutan" on your Grab or taxi app and find UBNHS easily.
