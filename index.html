UBNHS Hosts Annual Science and Technology Fair 2024

(UBNHS) held its much-anticipated Annual Science and Technology Fair. The event showcased innovative projects from students across all grade levels, focusing on the theme "Sustainable Innovations for a Better Future." Highlights included eco-friendly engineering designs, tech-driven learning tools, and health solutions.

UBNHS National Reading Month with Literacy Programs

In celebration of National Reading Month, Upper Bicutan National High School launched a month-long series of literacy programs aimed at fostering a love for reading among students. Activities included storytelling sessions, book drives, and a "Read-a-thon" competition.

UBNHS Conducts Brigada Eskwela 2024

UBNHS held its annual Brigada Eskwela program, rallying students, parents, teachers, and community members to prepare the school for the new academic year. Activities included classroom repainting, gardening, and repairs of school facilities..

Our Mission & Vision

Building a brighter future through quality education and community development.

Our Mission

"To provide accessible, quality, and holistic education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values to become productive and responsible members of society, fostering lifelong learning and active citizenship."

Our Vision

"A leading institution of learning, empowering students to achieve their full potential and contribute positively to the community through excellence in education, innovation, and inclusivity."

School Rules and Regulations

Students are expected to attend school regularly and arrive on time. Excessive absences or tardiness will result in disciplinary action.

Students must wear the appropriate school uniform. Violations of the dress code will lead to disciplinary measures.

Students should show respect towards their peers, teachers, and school staff at all times. Bullying, harassment, and discrimination are strictly prohibited.

Mobile phones must be turned off during class hours and can only be used during breaks. Unauthorized use of mobile phones will result in confiscation.

Students must complete their own work and avoid cheating or plagiarism. Violations of academic integrity will lead to serious consequences.
