About Us

Upper Bicutan National High School (UBNHS) has been a cornerstone of quality education in the community. With a legacy of academic excellence, state-of-the-art facilities, and a dedicated faculty, UBNHS prepares students for a bright future.

  • Established in 2008
  • Over 1,200 students enrolled
  • Recognized for excellence in science and technology
  • Award-winning faculty and alumni
