Admissions

Your journey begins here

Admission Process

Our admission process is simple and streamlined. Follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Complete the online application form.
  • Step 2: Submit required documents.
  • Step 3: Attend an interview or assessment.
  • Step 4: Receive your admission decision.

Requirements

Ensure you meet the following requirements before applying:

  • Completed application form
  • High school transcripts
  • Standardized test scores (if applicable)
  • Letter of recommendation
  • Personal statement

Tuition Fees

Our tuition fees are competitive and designed to provide value:

  • Undergraduate programs: $10,000 per year
  • Graduate programs: $12,000 per year
  • Part-time programs: $500 per credit hour

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We offer a range of scholarships and financial aid options:

  • Merit-based scholarships
  • Need-based grants
  • Work-study programs
  • External funding opportunities

Contact our financial aid office for personalized guidance.
